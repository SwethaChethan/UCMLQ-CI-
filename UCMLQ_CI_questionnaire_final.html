Participant Information & Consent

This questionnaire asks about your experience listening to and enjoying music with a cochlear implant, including questions about specific instruments, singers, and musical styles. It is fairly long — most people take around 30–40 minutes to complete it — so feel free to take breaks and come back; your answers are saved automatically as you go.

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You can stop at any time, and you may skip any question you'd prefer not to answer. Choosing not to take part, or stopping partway through, will not affect your care or your relationship with your clinic, audiologist, or research team in any way.

Your responses are stored only on this device (in your browser) until you choose to export and share them — nothing is sent automatically to any server. Once you export your answers and share them with your clinician or research team, they will be kept confidential: stored securely, accessible only to the people involved in your care or this research, and identified by a response ID rather than your name in any reporting or publication. Your individual answers will never be shared in a way that identifies you personally without your separate, explicit permission.

If you have any questions about this questionnaire or how your information will be used, please ask your audiologist, clinician, or the research team before continuing.
